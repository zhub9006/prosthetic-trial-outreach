### Key Trial Gaps Identified

1. **Only 1 trial explicitly targets low-income populations** — all 513 others assume insured, stable participants.
2. **Zero Phase 3 pivotal trials** for novel prosthetic interfaces targeting underserved cohorts.
3. **No pediatric lower-limb prosthetic RCTs** — children's amputations have entirely different needs with no age-stratified trials.

## 3. Seven Proposed Outreach Strategies

### Strategy 1: "Bridging the Gap" — Community Trial Navigators — $90K/yr
**Gap:** Geographic and racial access disparity
- Hire 4 community health workers from South/West Side Chicago
- Conduct outreach at community centers, VA clinics, disability orgs
- Assist with ClinicalTrials.gov navigation and consent explanation
- Track enrollment demographics for parity

### Strategy 2: "Prosthetic Supply Corridor" — Pop-up DME — $15-50K/yr
**Gap:** Zero DME within 500m
- Monthly pop-up clinic in SRAL lobby with prosthetic manufacturer (Otto Bock, Fillauer)
- Same-day socket/liner adjustments, component demos, insurance navigation

### Strategy 3: "Warm Hands, Warm Access" — Thermal Comfort — $33K/yr
**Gap:** Winter thermal discomfort
- Heated waiting pavilion + prosthetic thermal kits (liner warmers, sock heaters)
- Partner with gyms for indoor warm-up spaces

### Strategy 4: "South Side Transit Pipeline" — CTA Paratransit + Tele-Trial — $60K/yr
**Gap:** 1+ bus transfer burden
- Door-to-door CTA paratransit to SRAL; tele-trial screening and follow-up
- Pre-paid Ventra cards for in-person visits

### Strategy 5: "Global Equity Bridge" — International Partnerships — $50K/yr
**Gap:** 80 nations with zero trial access
- MOUs with Colombia, India, Pakistan rehab centers
- Standardized <$500 protocol for parallel multi-national studies

### Strategy 6: "Mental Health and Acceptance" — Integrated Support — $20K/yr
**Gap:** 30-50% amputee depression rate
- PHQ-2 screening at all intake; monthly peer groups with Medrina
- Bilingual counselor for Hispanic/Latino participants

### Strategy 7: "Women, Children and Gender-Diverse" — Inclusive Protocols — $15K/yr
**Gap:** 90%+ male skew; zero pediatric trials
- Gender-neutral sizing and consent forms; Lurie Children's pediatric sub-study
- Requirement for sex/gender stratification in all new trial registrations
