#### 🔴 Key Gap: Industry-Led Trials Are Scarce
Only **13.7%** of trials are industry-sponsored. Device manufacturers (Ottobock, Össur, etc.) are not investing in FDA-regulated prosthetic clinical research at scale. Innovative prosthetic technologies enter the market with thin clinical evidence. Veterans and rural amputees depending on VA/DoD-funded trials (9.6%) have limited access.
