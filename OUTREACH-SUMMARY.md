## 3. Proposed Outreach Strategies

### Trial Recruitment
1. **Community Trial Navigator** — Screen patients at AbilityLab; target 20→35% recruitment
2. **Multilingual Fact Sheets** — Spanish, Chinese, Arabic, Polish, Vietnamese
3. **Telehealth Pre-Screening Portal** — Match users to trials remotely
4. **Industry-Academic Exchange** — Quarterly forum (73% academic vs 14% industry)
5. **Phase Transparency Initiative** — Clear phase labels; "Trial Readiness" indicator
6. **Rural Outreach** — VA, vocational rehab, mailed screening kits

### Site Accessibility
1. **ADA Transit Guide** — Accessible CTA routes for patient packets
2. **Sheraton Valet Coordination** — Dedicated drop-off for prosthetic users
3. **Tactile Paving Advocacy** — Push CTA/Chicago for 12 nearest stops
4. **Parking Pass Program** — Discounted day passes at Doubletree & Ogden Plaza
5. **Divvy Adaptive Pilot** — Hand-cycle/trike options near site

### Community Trust-Building
1. **Prosthetic User Advisory Council** — 10–15 diverse users advise on design
2. **Monthly "Trial Tales"** — Storytelling events at AbilityLab
3. **Faith/Community Partnerships** — Uptown, Andersonville, Edgewater
4. **Social Media** — #ProstheticTrials on Facebook, Instagram, Reddit
