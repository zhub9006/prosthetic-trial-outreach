### 1.2 Phase Distribution — Critical Gap

| Phase | Count | Percentage |
|---|---|---|
| NA (pre-phase / device feasibility) | 69–76 | 76–80% |
| Unknown phase/status | 15–22 | 16–22% |
| Phase 2 (early efficacy) | 2–3 | 2–3% |
| Phase 3 (pivotal efficacy) | 2 | <2% |
| Phase 4 (post-market surveillance) | 2 | <2% |

**Key finding:** 76–80% of trials are pre-phase, and no more than 3–4 advanced-phase trials exist across the entire landscape. This is the single most significant gap — the field lacks pivotal efficacy evidence needed to move novel prosthetic technologies from bench to standard-of-care.

## Part 3 — Proposed Outreach Strategies

### Strategy 1: Mobile Prosthetist / DME Van
Monthly mobile prosthetic van with licensed prosthetist for fittings, adjustments, and repairs. Target: 50+ users/month. Cost: $15K–$20K/yr.

### Strategy 2: Language & Cultural Access
Bilingual consent forms (Spanish, Polish, Chinese, Arabic) + bilingual community coordinators. Cost: $5K–$10K/yr.

### Strategy 3: FQHC Partnerships
Formal MOUs with South/West Side FQHCs for screening + referral pipeline. Cost: $2K–$5K/yr.

### Strategy 4: Rural Tele-Outreach Program
Virtual pre-screening and telehealth follow-up modeled on VA WEST trial. Cost: $10K–$25K/yr.

### Strategy 5: Pediatric & Elderly Inclusive Trial Design
Inclusive age range 10–80; child-friendly engagement; elder-inclusive assessments. Cost: ~$50K incremental.

### Strategy 6: Peer Navigator Program
Train 3–5 peer navigators from the prosthetic user community for trial awareness and enrollment. Cost: $25K–$35K/yr.

### Strategy 7: Resolve Parking & DME Gap
Negotiate accessible parking with nearest 2 garages; attract 1 DME vendor to SRAL or adjacent retail. Cost: $3K–$10K/yr.

## Part 4 — Recommended Next Steps

1. Prioritize Strategy 1 and Strategy 6 — highest-impact gaps with moderate cost.
2. Run a pilot outreach at SRAL in Q3–Q4 2026, targeting 20–30 prosthetic users.
3. Push for Phase 2/3 trial design — 76% pre-phase trials need pivotal efficacy data.
4. Advocate for NIBIB inclusion in NIH prosthetic funding — only 1 NIH-funded trial exists.
5. Evaluate SRAL as a regional hub — transit score 10/10 makes it ideal for a prosthetic user network.
