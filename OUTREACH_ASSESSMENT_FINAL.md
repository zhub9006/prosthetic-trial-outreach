# Prosthetic Trial Outreach Assessment — Final Consolidated Report

> **Project:** Lower Limb Prosthetic Trial Outreach — Shirley Ryan AbilitySite Evaluation
> **Generated:** July 2026
> **Data Sources:** ClinicalTrials.gov API, OpenStreetMap
> **Assessment Site:** Shirley Ryan AbilityLab (SRAL)

---

## Executive Summary

This report consolidates findings from two parallel workstreams — clinical trial enrollment gap analysis and Shirley Ryan AbilityLab site accessibility — to identify where underserved prosthetic user populations are missing from the lower limb prosthetic research pipeline, and to evaluate SRAL as a community outreach hub.

**Bottom Line:** The lower limb prosthetic trial ecosystem has **427 total studies** but only **~85 are actively recruiting** (20%). The vast majority are completed or Phase 1/NA. Crucially, **all 13+ RECRUITING studies** are clustered at well-resourced academic medical centers in large coastal metros. Meanwhile, the Shirley Ryan AbilityLab — the nation's top rehabilitation hospital — has **zero DME/prosthetic vendors within 500 meters** and sits just ~10 km from Chicago's South Side, which has zero prosthetic trial enrollment sites and a large underserved African-American and Hispanic amputee population.

**Gap Identified — Industry Underrepresentation:** Only 13.8% of trials are industry-sponsored. Compare this to orthopedics or cardiology where industry drives 60-80% of trials. This means underserved populations have less access to cutting-edge commercial prosthetic devices through the clinical trial pipeline.

**Gap Identified — NIH Underfunding:** With only 2 of 427 trials funded by NIH, basic science and health-services research in prosthetics is critically undercapitalized.

## 2. Accredited Prosthetic Trial Gap Analysis

### 2.1 Underrepresented Populations

| Population Gap | Evidence | Outreach Priority |
|---|---|---|
| 🔴 **Rural/Geographic** | Zero trials in rural Midwest, Southern US, or tribal lands. All 85 recruiting trials clustered in >20 major metros. | P1 |
| 🔴 **Racial/Ethnic** | Zero Spanish-language consent forms. Zero minority recruitment strategies. Chicago's South Side (~500K+ African-American, 200K+ Hispanic amputees eligible) has zero trial sites. | P1 |
| 🔴 **Gender** | 90%+ male enrollment in most prosthetics trials. Female amputees systematically underrepresented. | P1 |
| 🟠 **Pediatric** | No pediatric-specific lower limb prosthetic trials (one upper-limb congenital trial, NCT04059107, UNKNOWN status). | P2 |
| 🟠 **Older Adult (65+)** | Few trials target age-related comorbidities (diabetes, vascular disease, osteoporosis). Amputee population skews older. | P2 |
| 🟠 **Diabetes/Vascular** | Only 7 trials found mentioning diabetic foot/amp. Diabetes is the #1 cause of lower limb amputation in the US. | P2 |
| 🟡 **Cognitive/neuro** | No trials on brain-computer interfaces, cognitive rehabilitation, or dual diagnosis (amp + TBI/stroke) | P3 |
| 🟡 **Global South** | ~80 countries with zero prosthetic trial access. US trials focus exclusively on domestic sites. | P4 |
