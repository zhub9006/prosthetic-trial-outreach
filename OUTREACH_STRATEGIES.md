# Outreach Strategies — 7 Proposed Actions

> **Shirley Ryan AbilityLab Prosthetic Trial Outreach Project** (zhub9006)
> Total Annual Operating Budget Estimate: **~$270K/yr** (~$315K first year)

---

## Strategy 1: "Bridging the Gap" — Community Trial Navigators ($135K-$180K/yr)

**Target Gap:** South/West Side Chicago — geographic & racial access
- Recruit 3-4 **Community Trial Navigators** from South Shore, Englewood, Austin ($45K/yr each)
- Train on trial eligibility, consent, transport logistics
- Partner with South Side Community Health Centers (Rosalind Franklin, Provident Hospital)
- Monthly **"Trial Café"** info sessions at community centers (not at SRAL)
- KPI: ≥15 new diverse participants/study/yr from South/West Side

---

## Strategy 2: "Prosthetic Supply Corridor" — Pop-up DME ($15K-$50K + setup)

**Target Gap:** ZERO DME/prosthetic vendors within 500m of SRAL
- **Option A:** Monthly pop-up DME clinic in SRAL lobby ($15K/yr) — Össur/Ottobock/Hanger Clinic
- **Option B:** Permanent prosthetic access station ($50K setup + $30K/yr)
- Free socket checks & padding adjustments at SRAL
- DME manufacturer sponsorship (tax-deductible)
- KPI: ≥50 DME interactions/month; 10% reduction in "prosthesis issue" cancel rate

---

## Strategy 3: "Warm Hands, Warm Access" — Heated Area + Winter Kits ($33K/yr)

**Target Gap:** Chicago winter wind chill <0°F; thermal discomfort = PRIMARY barrier
- Heated waiting area in SRAL check-in zone ($25K one-time + $8K/yr HVAC/electric)
- **Winter Prosthetic Thermal Kit:** wool socks, hand warmers, insulated bags, thermal liner, gait guide
- CTA winter transit passes ($60/participant)
- Indoor alternatives for outdoor assessments Nov-Mar
- KPI: ≥85% winter attendance rate (vs ~60% baseline est.)

---

## Strategy 4: "South Side Transit Pipeline" — CTA Shuttle + Tele-Trial ($60K/yr)

**Target Gap:** South/West Siders need 1+ bus transfers to reach SRAL
- Dedicated CTA Access-A-Ride shuttle route ($60K/yr subsidized rides)
- **Tele-Trial Hybrid Model:**
  - Visit 1 (in-person): Consent + baseline + device fitting at SRAL
  - Visits 2-4 (telehealth): Remote check-in + device data
  - Final Visit (in-person): Outcomes assessment
- Pre-loaded tablets for participants ($15K one-time for 30 tablets)
- **"Team Trials"** — 2-4 neighborhood participants attend together
- KPI: ≥40% no-show reduction for tele-trial arm; 15 tele-trial participants/study/yr

---

## Strategy 5: "Global Equity Bridge" — International Partnerships ($50K + $50K/yr)

**Target Gap:** ~80 nations have ZERO prosthetic trials
- Sign MOUs with research groups in **Colombia** (Bogotá), **India** (Delhi/Mumbai), **Pakistan** (Lahore/Karachi)
- Design low-cost protocol adaptations (<$500/protocol)
- Shared REDCap data protocol
- Pilot joint K1-K2 powered knee study in South Asia (adapted NCT06194838 model)
- Apply PCORI + NIH Fogarty International Center funding
- KPI: 3+ MOUs in 18mo; 1 joint study in 24mo; 5 non-US participants/study

---

## Strategy 6: "Mental Health & Acceptance" — PHQ-2 + Peer Group ($20K/yr)

**Target Gap:** 30-50% amputees experience depression; excluded by most protocols
- Universal PHQ-2 screening at trial intake
- Partner with **Medrina** for culturally adapted mental health triage
- Monthly peer support group (normalize trial participation as rehab, not "experimentation")
- **Trial Ambassador** program — 5-10 past trial participants as ambassadors
- Offer trial as part of standard rehab (not separate "research")
- KPI: 25% intake PHQ-2 screening; 10 peer group attendees/month; quarterly Ambassador events

---

## Strategy 7: "Women, Children & Gender-Diverse" — Equal-Platform + Pediatric ($35K + $15K/yr)

**Target Gap:** ~90%+ male adult-traumatic-amputation skew across all 427 trials
- **Equal-Platform Protocol:** mandatory 30% enrollment minimum for women & family units
- **Lurie Children's Pediatric Sub-Study** ($35K start): ages 10-17; child life specialist; gamified outcomes
- **Family-Friendly Visit:** sibling care, refreshments, play area during trial waiting
- Recruit via Chicago Women's Health Center + Gladys Academy
- KPI: 30% female enrollment; 10 pediatric/yr; 1 gender-diverse/study; pediatric protocol in 12mo
