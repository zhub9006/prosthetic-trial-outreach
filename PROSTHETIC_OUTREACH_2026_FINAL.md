## 3. Outreach Strategy Recommendations

### Strategy 1: Mobile Prosthetics Outreach Van

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Problem** | Zero DME vendors within 500 m; many prosthetic users lack transportation |
| **Solution** | Monthly mobile prosthetist van staffed by volunteer O&P clinicians |
| **Target** | Uninsured / underinsured prosthetic users, especially South/West Side referrals |
| **Estimated cost** | ~$15K/yr (van lease + clinician stipends) |
| **Metrics** | Number of fittings, follow-up compliance, patient satisfaction |

### Strategy 2: Language Access Program

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Problem** | 0 trials offer Spanish consent; Chicago has large Spanish-speaking communities |
| **Solution** | Bilingual (EN/ES) outreach coordinators; Spanish-language trial materials |
| **Target** | Hispanic/Latino prosthetic users on the Near North Side and Pilsen/Little Village |
| **Estimated cost** | ~$8K/yr |

### Strategy 3: FQHC / Community Health Center Partnerships

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Problem** | No trials reach patients where they get primary care |
| **Solution** | Embed referral workflows at Federally Qualified Health Centers on the South and West Sides |
| **Target** | Low-income, minority prosthetic users who are systematically excluded |
| **Estimated cost** | ~$5K/yr |

### Strategy 4: Rural Tele-Outreach

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Problem** | 0 rural trial sites; prosthetic users in rural IL/IN have no local options |
| **Solution** | Virtual consultation-and-enrollment via VA telehealth; partner with existing VA rural sites |
| **Target** | Rural veteran prosthetic users |
| **Estimated cost** | ~$10K/yr |

### Strategy 5: Pediatric Prosthetic Trial Design

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Problem** | No prosthetics trials include children |
| **Solution** | Pilot a child-friendly trial with adapted consent, play-based outcomes, and parent navigation |
| **Target** | Children (2–17) with congenital or acquired limb differences |
| **Estimated cost** | ~$20K/yr |

### Strategy 6: Civic / Nonprofit Sponsorship Drive

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Problem** | 0 civic/nonprofit-funded prosthetic trials; sponsor landscape is entirely industry/government |
| **Solution** | Recruit philanthropic and community foundation sponsors to fund trials with equity mandates |
| **Target** | Grants from Robert Wood Johnson Foundation, OWH, NIA, community foundations |
| **Estimated cost** | ~$3K/yr |
