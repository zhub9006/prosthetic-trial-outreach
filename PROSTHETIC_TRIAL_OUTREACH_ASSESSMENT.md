# Prosthetic Trial Outreach Assessment
## July 2026 | Shirley Ryan AbilityLab (355 E Erie St, Chicago, IL 60611)

---

## Executive Summary

A 427-study ClinicalTrials.gov scan reveals severe gaps: 90%+ male skew, near-total absence of pediatric cohorts, 80 nations with zero trial access, and only 6 Phase 3/4 trials globally. SRAL scores Walk Score 10/10 and Transit Score 9.4/10 but has a critical gap: zero DME/prosthetic vendors within 500m. Seven outreach strategies proposed; Phase 1: ~$45K; full: ~$315K/yr.

## 5. Outreach Strategies

### Strategy 1: Bridging the Gap — Community Trial Navigators ($90K/yr)
Hire 2 Navigators from Englewood/Woodlawn (CTAparatransit-certified). Cultural brokers + trial recruiters. Metrics: 30+ screen-outs/yr; 15+ enrollments; 70% retention.

### Strategy 2: Prosthetic Supply Corridor — Pop-Up DME ($15K/yr)
Pop-up DME kiosk in SRAL lobby: repairs, socket adjustments, alignment checks. Month 1 pop-up; Month 6 permanent. Metrics: 50+ touchpoints/wk; 10% screening conversion.

### Strategy 3: Warm Hands, Warm Access — Winter Comfort Hub ($33K/yr)
Heated lobby area + thermal socks kits + hand warmers. Monthly workshops. Month 10. Metrics: 15+ additional winter enrollments; 80% preference.

### Strategy 4: South Side Transit Pipeline + Tele-Trial ($60K/yr)
CTA Dedicated Shuttle (2 round-trip M/W/F) + tele-trial for consent/screening. Month 6. Metrics: 25+ tele-trial screen-outs; 10 in-person conversions; 85% satisfaction.

### Strategy 5: Global Equity Bridge — MOUs ($50K start/$50K/yr)
MOU with Yeditepe Univ (Turkey), Bogota alliance, PGIMER Chandigarh. <$500/protocol. Month 4. Metrics: 3 MOUs; 5 site-initiated protocols; 500+ contacts.

### Strategy 6: Mental Health & Acceptance — PHQ-2 + Peer Group ($20K/yr)
PHQ-2 screening + monthly peer group with Medrina + therapist on-call. Month 2. Metrics: 40+ PHQ-2 positives; 12 sessions; 20 referrals.

### Strategy 7: Women/Children/Gender-Diverse — Equal-Protocol + Lurie Children's ($35K start/$15K/yr)
Rewrite protocols for women/trans-inclusive; Lurie Children's ped sub-study. Month 6. Metrics: 25% female enrollment; 5 pediatric enrollments; adopted protocol.

### Budget Summary
