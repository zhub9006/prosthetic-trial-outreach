## Top 8 Proposed Outreach Strategies

| # | Strategy | Est. Cost | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Mobile DME Unit** — Monthly mobile prosthetist van for uninsured/underinsured | ~$15K/yr | #1 site-level barrier; brings prosthetic care to community |
| 2 | **Language Access Program** — Spanish, Polish, Arabic, Chinese consent forms + bilingual coordinators | ~$25K | Chicago's diverse immigrant communities lack-language-accessible trials |
| 3 | **FQHC Partnerships** — Partner with South/West Side Federally Qualified Health Centers for screening & referral | ~$40K/yr | Connects trial to diverse, underserved patient pipeline |
| 4 | **Rural Tele-Outreach** — Virtual consults with VA rural sites; tele-rehabilitation for mobility-limited patients | ~$30K/yr | Eliminates geographic barrier; leverages VA infrastructure |
| 5 | **Pediatric Trial Design** — Child-friendly prosthetic trial with flexible age ranges and family-centered outcomes | ~$250K startup | Zero pediatric trials exist; children are profoundly underserved |
| 6 | **SRAL On-Site Resource Corner** — DME samples, insurance navigation, walk-in socket adjustments | ~$10K startup | Immediate, tangible benefit to prosthetic users at SRAL |
| 7 | **ADA Parking Solutions** — Reserved ADA spots + shuttle service to/from SRAL | ~$5K | Addresses site-specific mobility barrier |
| 8 | **Adaptive Cycling Program** — Partner with Divvy for adaptive cycles at nearby stations | ~$10K startup | Promotes active mobility; leverages existing Divvy infrastructure |
