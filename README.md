# Prosthetic Trial Outreach Project

Community outreach assessment for lower limb prosthetic trials, identifying enrollment gaps and evaluating Shirley Ryan AbilityLab as a community outreach site for prosthetic users.

**Repository:** https://github.com/zhub9006/prosthetic-trial-outreach  
**Last Updated:** July 2026  
**Active Trial:** NCT06160882 (powered prosthesis for TF osseointegration recipients, RECRUITING, 6 participants)

---

## Overview

This project assesses the current landscape of lower limb prosthetic clinical trials on ClinicalTrials.gov (427 total trials), identifies gaps in underserved population enrollment, and evaluates the Shirley Ryan AbilityLab (355 E Erie St, Chicago, IL 60611) as a potential community outreach hub for prosthetic users.

## Key Findings Summary

### Clinical Trial Gaps (427 Trials Analyzed)

| Finding | Detail |
|---|---|
| 🔴 **Industry Underrepresentation** | Only **19%** of trials are industry-sponsored — limits tech access for underserved populations |
| 🔴 **Phase Pipeline Gap** | **96%** of trials are in NA or Unknown phase — no rigorous later-stage efficacy testing |
| 🔴 **NIH Underfunding** | Only **2** of 427 trials are NIH-funded |
| 🔴 **Rural Invisibility** | Zero trials in rural Midwest, Southern US, or tribal lands |
| 🔴 **Racial/Ethnic Gaps** | No Spanish-language consent; no minority recruitment; Chicago's South Side has ZERO trial sites |
| 🔴 **Gender Gap** | 90%+ male enrollment in most prosthetics trials |
| 🔴 **Pediatric Absence** | No pediatric-specific prosthetic trials found |
| 🔴 **Global South** | ~80 nations with zero prosthetic trial access |

## Highlighted Outreach Strategies

### P1 (Immediate — ~$111K/yr)
1. **Patient Navigation & Transportation** — CTA reduced-fare cards, Divvy Adaptive, ride-share vouchers, patient navigator ($10K/yr)
2. **Data Transparency Dashboard** — Open-source on GitHub showing trial demographics, gaps, outcomes (3 months)
3. **Community Advisory Board (CAB)** — 10-15 prosthetic users + CHWs from underserved populations (1 month to form)
4. **Mandatory Diversity Benchmarks** — ≥25% female, ≥30% minority, ≥15% Medicaid (12 months)

### P2 (6-12 months — ~$380K/yr)
5. **Mobile Screening Events** — VA/FQHC mobile units in rural Midwest & Chicago South Side
6. **Industry-Academic Pipeline** — Pro bono device donations + deployment to underserved sites
7. **DME Integration at SRAL** — Prosthetic access station within 500m (currently 0) — CRITICAL GAP

### P3 (6-12 months — ~$30K/yr)
8. **Older Adult Programming** — Caregiver stipends, in-home assessments, senior center partnerships
9. **Tele-Trial Hub** — Virtual visits, mail-in device checks, team trials

### P4 (12+ months — ~$350K/yr)
10. **South Side Permanent Office** — 24/7 PR for African American/Hispanic communities (~10km from SRAL)
11. **Rural Outreach via VA** — Mobile prosthetics labs quarterly to VA clinics in IL, MO, IN
12. **Mental Health Integration** — Peer support mentors at Medrina (~605m) + support groups
