- Less funding for community outreach and participant compensation
- Rural and underserved populations have less access to trial-provided devices

- **Underserved groups are missing from the tiny Phase 2-4 pipeline** where diversity matters most

## 5. Proposed Outreach Strategies

### Priority 1 — Immediate (Year 1, ~$141K)

#### P1.1: Patient Navigation & Transportation Program
- **Cost:** ~$10K/yr
- **Action:** Provide CTA reduced-fare cards, Divvy Adaptive (2-wheeled scooters), Lyft ride-share vouchers for patients who can't use transit
- **Target:** Medicaid/uninsured prosthetic users, elderly patients, those with cognitive barriers
- **Metric:** Transport no-show rate reduction from baseline (~30%) to <15%

#### P1.2: Open-Source Data Transparency Dashboard
- **Cost:** ~$5K (dev tools) + ~$5K/yr hosting
- **Action:** Build a GitHub-hosted dashboard showing real-time ClinicalTrials.gov data: trial demographics, enrollment gaps, sponsor phases, geographic distribution
- **Target:** Community members, advocates, researchers
- **Metric:** Dashboard unique visitors, community engagement metrics

#### P1.3: Community Advisory Board (CAB)
- **Cost:** ~$20K/yr (stipends + facilitation)
- **Action:** Recruit 10-15 prosthetic users from underserved populations (racial minorities, rural, elderly, women) to advise trial design and outreach
- **Target:** South Side communities, rural veterans, women with limb loss
- **Metric:** CAB meetings quarterly; ≥50% members from underrepresented groups

#### P1.4: Mandatory Diversity Benchmarks for All SRAL Trials
- **Cost:** ~$76K/yr (staff time + tracking)
- **Action:** Require ≥25% female, ≥30% minority enrollment; ≥15% Medicaid/Medicare; geographic diversity tracking
- **Target:** All SRAL-affiliated trials (currently NCT06160882 enrolling 6 participants)
- **Metric:** Enrollment demographics vs. benchmarks; audit quarterly

### Priority 2 — 6-12 Months (Year 2, ~$380K/yr)

#### P2.1: Mobile Screening Events
- **Cost:** ~$150K/yr (van + staff)
- **Action:** Partner with VA and FQHC mobile units to screen prospective participants at Chicago South Side clinics (Woodlawn, Englewood) and rural Midwest VA sites
- **Target:** Communities 10+ km from SRAL with highest amputation rates
- **Metric:** # of screenings per quarter; conversion to trial enrollment

#### P2.2: Industry-Academic Device Donation Pipeline
- **Cost:** ~$80K/yr (negotiation + logistics)
- **Action:** Negotiate pro bono device donations from industry sponsors; deploy to underserved trial sites
- **Target:** Industry-sponsored trials that currently exclude low-income participants
- **Metric:** # of devices donated; # of outreach sites receiving devices

#### P2.3: DME/Prosthetic Access Station at SRAL
- **Cost:** ~$150K (buildout) + ~$50K/yr (staff)
- **Action:** Partner with a DME provider to establish a prosthetic fitting/assessment station within the SRAL campus (currently 0 within 500m)
- **Target:** All prosthetic users adjacent to SRAL
- **Metric:** # of fittings per quarter; reduction in travel time for device access

### Priority 3 — 6-12 Months (Year 2, ~$30K/yr)

#### P3.1: Older Adult Programming
- **Cost:** ~$12K/yr
- **Action:** Caregiver stipends, in-home prosthetic assessments, partnerships with senior centers on the North Side
- **Target:** Amputees 65+ (growing segment; highest fall risk)
- **Metric:** # of older adults enrolled; satisfaction scores

#### P3.2: Tele-Trial Virtual Hub
- **Cost:** ~$18K/yr (platform + connectivity)
- **Action:** Virtual trial visits, mail-in device integrity checks, team-based trial participation for rural patients
- **Target:** Rural Midwest amputees (40%+ of US amputee population)
- **Metric:** # of tele-participants; retention rates vs. in-person

### Priority 4 — 12+ Months (Year 3+, ~$350K/yr)

#### P4.1: South Side Permanent Outreach Office
- **Cost:** ~$300K/yr (lease + staff)
- **Action:** Establish a staffed satellite office on Chicago's South Side (Englewood or Woodlawn, ~10 km from SRAL) targeting African American and Hispanic communities
- **Target:** Highest amputation prevalence communities with zero current trial access
- **Metric:** # of South Side participants per trial; community trust metrics

#### P4.2: Rural Outreach via VA Clinics
- **Cost:** ~$50K/yr (travel + mobile lab)
- **Action:** Quarterly mobile prosthetics labs to VA clinics in Springfield IL, St. Louis MO, Indianapolis IN
- **Target:** Rural veterans (2× amputation rate, limited trial access)
- **Metric:** # of rural veterans screened/enrolled

#### P4.3: Mental Health Integration
- **Cost:** ~$200K/yr
- **Action:** Embed peer support mentors at nearby Medrina (~605m); support groups for prosthetic adjustment, grief, depression screening
- **Target:** All prosthetic users — depression is #1 reason for prosthetic abandonment (30-40%)
- **Metric:** PHQ-9 scores pre/post; prosthetic abandonment rates; group attendance
