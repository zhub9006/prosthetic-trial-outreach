### 3.2 Detailed Strategy Descriptions

#### Strategy 1: Community Health Worker (CHW) Bridge Program
- Partner with 3–5 FQHCs on Chicago's South & West Sides
- Train CHWs to conduct initial prosthetic screening and trial eligibility referrals
- CHWs accompany participants to first visit; provide navigation support through consent/assent processes
- **Primary gaps:** Low-income, non-English speakers, rural residents

#### Strategy 2: Mobile Prosthetic Screening Clinics
- Deploy mobile rehab units to underserved neighborhoods (Austin, Englewood, South Shore)
- Offer free prosthetic evaluations, gait assessments, and trial eligibility screenings
- Collect data on demographics of attendees to refine outreach
- **Primary gaps:** Rural, low-income, wheelchair users, elderly 75+

#### Strategy 3: Multicultural & Multilingual Outreach
- Partner with faith-based organizations (churches, mosques, temples) in diverse neighborhoods
- Translate trial materials into Spanish, Polish, Mandarin, and Arabic at minimum
- Host informational sessions in community gathering spaces (not clinical settings)
- **Primary gaps:** Non-English speakers, immigrant communities

#### Strategy 4: Telehealth Eligibility Screening
- Develop remote screening platform for initial trial eligibility assessment
- Reduces travel burden for rural and wheelchair-dependent potential participants
- Virtual informed consent with interpretive services available on-demand
- **Primary gaps:** Rural, wheelchair users, elderly, low-income

#### Strategy 5: Peer Mentor Network
- Recruit diverse amputee mentors (varying ages, amputation types, socioeconomic backgrounds)
- Pair mentors with potential participants for trial orientation and support
- Mentors provide lived-experience perspective on trial participation
- **Primary gaps:** All underserved groups

#### Strategy 6: Transportation & Practical Supports
- Provide CTA transit credits or rideshare vouchers for trial visits
- Offer childcare stipends for caregivers accompanying participants
- Create parking voucher program for trial participants at SRAL
- **Primary gaps:** Low-income, rural, wheelchair users, caregivers

#### Strategy 7: Data Equity Audit
- Systematically review all 425 trials for exclusionary criteria (age, comorbidity, language, geography)
- Publish findings and advocate for inclusive eligibility criteria in future trial designs
- Engage disability rights organizations in audit process
- **Primary gaps:** Systemic — all underserved groups

#### Strategy 8: Prosthetic Access Hub at Shirley Ryan AbilityLab
- Convert underutilized space to free community meeting room + quarterly community days
- Host open houses, educational workshops, and peer support groups
- Ground-floor accessible entrance with clear wayfinding signage
- **Primary gaps:** All underserved groups; specifically addresses neighborhood welcome gap
