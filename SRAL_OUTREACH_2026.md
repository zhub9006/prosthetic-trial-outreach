# Shirley Ryan AbilityLab Outreach Assessment — July 2026

> **Project**: Prosthetic Trial Outreach Assessment
> **Site**: Shirley Ryan AbilityLab, 355 E Erie St, Chicago, IL 60611
> **Purpose**: Evaluate site suitability for community outreach to prosthetic users and identify gaps in lower-limb prosthetic trial enrollment.

---

## 1. Clinical Trial Landscape — Lower-Limb Prosthetics

### 1.1 Overall Landscape
ClinicalTrials.gov lists **104 total** lower-limb prosthetic/amputation studies. Filtering to actively recruiting trials yields approximately **40 active studies** (including RECRUITING, ACTIVE_NOT_RECRUITING, and NOT_YET_RECRUITING).

### 1.2 Enrollment Trends by Sponsor Type (40 Active Recruiting Trials)

| Sponsor Type | Count | Share | Implication |
|---|---|---|---|
| **INDUSTRY** (medical device companies, Össur, etc.) | 11 | 28% | Device-focused; limited community reach |
| **OTHER** (universities, hospitals, non-profits) | 23 | 58% | Academically driven; some community programs |
| **FED** (NIH, VA, CDC, etc.) | 5 | 13% | Public health — but extremely thin for underserved populations |
| **OTHER_GOV** | 1 | 2% | Minimal government-sponsored research |

**Key Gap**: Federal and government-funded trials are dramatically under-represented. NIH/VA-funded trials that would serve underserved populations (rural, low-income, minority) are nearly absent.

### 1.3 Enrollment Trends by Phase (40 Active Recruiting Trials)

| Phase | Count | Share |
|---|---|---|
| **Phase 3** | 1 | 2.5% |
| **Phase 2** | **0** | **0%** |
| **Phase 1** | **0** | **0%** |
| **Unknown** | 8 | 20% |
| **NA** (device/device-failure) | 31 | 78% |

**Key Gap**: There are **zero Phase 1 or Phase 2 trials** for active recruiting lower-limb prosthetic studies. The pipeline for early-stage innovation is stalled.

### 1.4 Geographic Distribution (All 104 Studies)

| Country | Count |

## 4. Proposed Outreach Strategies

1. **Mobile DME Unit** — Monthly prosthetics van at SRAL for fittings, ~$15K/yr
2. **Language Access** — Spanish consent forms, bilingual navigators
3. **FQHC Partnerships** — Screen/referral pipeline on South & West Sides
4. **Rural Tele-Outreach** — Virtual consults linked to VA rural sites
5. **Pediatric Trial** — Child-friendly design with Shriner's & Lurie Children's
6. **CHW Ambassador Program** — Empower community health workers as trial ambassadors
7. **ADA Parking & Wayfinding** — Temporary spots, signage, CTA coordination
8. **Nonprofit Funding** — Pursue grants for civic/nonprofit-sponsored trials
