## 3 · Proposed Outreach Strategies

### Strategy 1 — "Bridging the Gap" Community Trial Navigators ($90,000/yr)

- **Target:** South/West Side Chicago (75th–95th St), where 68% of Chicago's amputee population resides but <5% of trials recruit
- **Action:** Hire 2 full-time Community Trial Navigators from target neighborhoods; partner with community health centers (Woodlawn, Englewood, Roseland)
- **Success Metric:** 30% increase in South/West Side enrollment within 18 months

### Strategy 2 — "Prosthetic Supply Corridor" — Pop-up DME Station ($15,000–50,000)

- **Target:** Zero DME within 500 m
- **Action:** Quarterly pop-up DME fitting clinic in SRAL lobby (lower-cost, $15K/yr) OR negotiate permanent 10×10 ft. station with Össur/Ottobock representative ($50K setup)
- **Success Metric:** 50+ fittings/quarter; 80% conversion to trial screening

### Strategy 3 — "Warm Hands, Warm Access" — Heated Waiting Area ($33,000/yr)

- **Target:** Thermal discomfort barrier (validated by NCT07215442 thermal comfort study)
- **Action:** Install heated waiting area with thermal prosthetic kits (sock liners, gel pads, antiperspirant); partner with Northwestern's HVAC for pilot
- **Success Metric:** 25% reduction in no-show rate during Nov–Mar

### Strategy 4 — "South Side Transit Pipeline" — CTA Paratransit Shuttle ($60,000/yr)

- **Target:** 1+ bus transfer required for South/West Siders to reach SRAL
- **Action:** Contract CTA Access-Transportation for 3x/week shuttle from 79th St Red Line to SRAL; tele-trial model for initial screening
- **Success Metric:** 40% of new enrollees from South Side; shuttle utilization ≥70%

### Strategy 5 — "Global Equity Bridge" — International Partnerships ($50K start / $50K/yr)

- **Target:** 80 nations with zero trial access
- **Action:** MOUs with Universidad del Rosario (Colombia), AIIMS (India), PIMS (Pakistan) for low-cost protocol adoption (<$500/site); shared IRB via CIHR
- **Success Metric:** 3 active international sites within 24 months

### Strategy 6 — "Mental Health & Acceptance" — Peer Group Program ($20,000/yr)

- **Target:** 30–50% of amputees experience depression; 75% treatment gap
- **Action:** Monthly peer support group co-facilitated with Dr. Medrina (psychiatry, 401 N Michigan, ~300 m); PHQ-2 screening embedded in trial consent; CBT referral pathway
- **Success Metric:** 60% retention increase; screening completion >90%

### Strategy 7 — "Women, Children & Gender-Diverse" — Inclusive Protocol ($35K start / $15K/yr)

- **Target:** 90%+ male skew; pediatric absence; gender-diverse exclusion
- **Action:** Revise SRAL protocols to equal-platform recruitment (50/50 gender split); partner with Lurie Children's Hospital (1.5 mi) for pediatric sub-study; gender-affirming prosthesis track
- **Success Metric:** 40% female enrollment; 1 pediatric sub-study active within 12 months
