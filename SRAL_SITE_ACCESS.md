## Site Assessment Summary

**Shirley Ryan AbilityLab is an EXCELLENT site for prosthetic trial outreach BECAUSE:**
- World-class prosthetics research (active NCT06160882)
- 10/10 Walk Score + 9.4/10 Transit Score — easiest Chicago site to reach
- 48 transit stops, 5 Divvy stations, water taxi access
- Adjacent hospital (Galter), VA clinic (~300m), urgent care
- 4 Walgreens (incl. 24-hr), Whole Foods, multiple dining
- 3 parking garages + 24/7 DoubleTree option

**BUT FOR UNDERSERVED USERS, these gaps must be addressed:**
- No DME on-site (zero within 500m)
- No free/disability parking
- No tactile paving at bus stops
- Winter thermal barrier
- Financial barriers (parking, transit)

**Mitigation IS possible** with the 7 outreach strategies in PROSTHETIC_OUTREACH_PLAN.md
