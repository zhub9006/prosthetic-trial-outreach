## 3. Outreach Strategies

1. **Mobile DME Unit** ($15K/yr) — monthly prosthetist van at SRAL for socket adjustments, insurance navigation, emergency repairs.
2. **Language Access Program** — Spanish/Polish/Arabic/Chinese materials + bilingual coordinators.
3. **FQHC Partnerships** — referral agreements with South/West Side health centers.
4. **Rural Tele-Outreach** — virtual subprotocols with VA rural sites.
5. **Pediatric Trial Design** — child-friendly prosthetic trial ages 8–70.
6. **Community Advisory Board** — 8–12 diverse prosthetic users, quarterly meetings.
