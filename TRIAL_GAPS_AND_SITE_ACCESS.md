## 8. Proposed Outreach Strategies

### Strategy 1: "Bridging the Gap" — Community Trial Navigators ($90K/yr)
- Hire 2-3 navigators from South/West Side Chicago
- Deploy to churches, health fairs, amputee support groups, VA centers
- Bilingual/bicultural; train on trial literacy & SRAL referral pathways
- **Goal:** 30% increase in screened participants from historically excluded ZIP codes in 12 months

### Strategy 2: "Prosthetic Supply Corridor" — Pop-up DME at SRAL ($15K–$50K/yr)
- Lease SRAL lobby space for DME vendor 2 days/week
- OR permanent prosthetic fitting station for trial participants
- Eliminates >500 m barrier for follow-up visits
- **Goal:** 100% trial completion rate for device adjustment needs

### Strategy 3: "Warm Hands, Warm Access" — Heated Waiting & Thermal Kits ($33K/yr + $25K one-time)
- Heated covered waiting area at SRAL
- Seasonal prosthetic thermal kits for participants
- Addresses NCT07215442 finding: thermal discomfort is primary winter barrier
- **Goal:** 30% improvement in winter retention

### Strategy 4: "South Side Transit Pipeline" — CTA Paratransit Shuttle ($60K/yr)
- Negotiate CTA paratransit contract SRAL↔South/West Side
- OR tele-trial model with mobile assessment units
- Target: 50+ new rural/South Side participants/year

### Strategy 5: "Global Equity Bridge" — International Partnerships ($50K start + $50K/yr)
- MOUs with institutions in Colombia, India, Pakistan
- <$500-cost protocol designs for low-resource settings
- Shared data agreements for global representation

### Strategy 6: "Mental Health & Acceptance" — Integrated Peer Support ($20K/yr)
- PHQ-2 depression screening at SRAL intake (30-50% amputee prevalence)
- Monthly peer support group with Medrina (~450 m away)
- Peer navigators with lived limb-loss experience

### Strategy 7: "Women, Children & Gender-Diverse" — Equal-Platform Protocol ($35K start + $15K/yr)
- Rewrite inclusion criteria to explicitly include women, children, gender-diverse participants
- Pediatric sub-study with Lurie Children's Hospital
- Gender-affirming prosthetic trial addendum

**Total estimated annual operational budget: ~$315,000/yr ($300K first year including one-time costs)**
